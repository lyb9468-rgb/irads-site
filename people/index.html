Yu Bin Lee, Ph.D. / 이유빈 is a senior researcher at the Korea Institute of Toxicology (KIT, 국가독성과학연구소, 독성연). His research focuses on human-relevant in vitro and microphysiological systems for predicting immune-mediated toxicity of advanced biopharmaceuticals.
