Principal Investigator

Yu Bin Lee, Ph.D.

Senior Researcher, Korea Institute of Toxicology

Yu Bin Lee, Ph.D. is a senior researcher at the Korea Institute of Toxicology. His research focuses on human-relevant in vitro and microphysiological systems for predicting immune-mediated toxicity of advanced biopharmaceuticals. His work integrates tissue engineering, immune-competent model systems, and translational safety assessment.
