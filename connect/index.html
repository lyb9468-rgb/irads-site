Connect

We welcome thoughtful inquiries from prospective students, academic collaborators, institutional partners, and industry researchers interested in human-relevant immune toxicology and translational safety assessment.

For Prospective Students

Students interested in tissue engineering, immune biology, toxicology, imaging, or computational interpretation are welcome to reach out with a brief research interest statement and CV.

Curiosity, reliability, and careful experimental thinking are valued more than prior expertise in every method.

For Collaborators

We are open to collaborations involving advanced biopharmaceutical safety, immune-mediated toxicity, human-relevant model systems, multi-organ interpretation, and translational assay development.

For Institutional or Industry Partners

We are interested in projects that connect mechanistic toxicology, human-relevant assay development, and translational decision-making for emerging therapeutic modalities.

Contact Information

Please include your name, affiliation, email, research interests, and a concise message when reaching out.
