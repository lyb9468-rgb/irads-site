Connect

We welcome thoughtful inquiries from prospective students, academic collaborators, institutional partners, companies, and other research partners.

This page is not a formal application portal. Please contact us by email with a brief and specific message.

Prospective students

Students interested in tissue engineering, immune biology, toxicology, imaging, or computational interpretation are welcome to reach out.

Please include a short research interest statement, CV, and any relevant experience.

Academic collaborators

We are open to collaborations involving human-relevant model systems, immune-mediated toxicity, advanced biopharmaceutical safety, multi-organ interpretation, and translational assay development.

Institutional, company, and other partners

We welcome discussions with institutional, company, and other partners interested in human-relevant safety assessment, immune-relevant in vitro platforms, and decision-oriented model development.
