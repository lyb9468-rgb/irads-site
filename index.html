IRADS

A framework-driven approach to immune-relevant safety assessment

We organize our research through IRADS, a layered framework that links therapeutic context, human-relevant model construction, immune-tissue interface sensing, integrated decision modeling, and translational application.

The goal is not only to measure toxicity, but to interpret how immune activation interacts with tissue vulnerability and how this information can guide safer therapeutic development.

What we build

Immune-competent human tissue models

3D and co-culture systems designed to capture tissue-level immune responses.

Multi-organ safety interpretation

Model systems and readouts for understanding organ-specific vulnerability and immune crosstalk.

Modality-aware decision frameworks

Assessment logic tailored to advanced biopharmaceuticals, cell therapies, nucleic acid therapeutics, and delivery platforms.

Publication-backed platforms and translational safety studies.
